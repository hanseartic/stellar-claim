Privacy
Your assets are safe here!
All stellar operations happen within the browser.
Your data is only sent to stellar's horizon server to submit the transactions to the ledger.
Your signature (secret) is not exposed
In order to claim the balances a transaction signed by your account must be submitted to stellar's network.
You can sign the transaction with Freighter to keep complete control over your secrets.
We only eat cookies
Your activity is not tracked in any form.
